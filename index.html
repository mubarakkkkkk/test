Notification of Copyright Infringement - DMCA

PollRepo is an Online Service Provider under the DMCA, respecting the legitimate rights of copyright owners. This section details the process for utilizing our notice and takedown procedure as required by the DMCA.

Notice to Owners of Copyrighted Works

If you believe your copyright has been infringed on our platform, please send a DMCA notice to PollRepo’s Designated Copyright Agent with the following information:

An electronic signature of the person authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed. Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, to be removed, or to which access should be disabled, along with sufficient information to locate the material. Contact information for the complaining party, such as an address, telephone number, and email address. A statement that the complaining party has a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that the complaining party is authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

Delivery of the Notice

Deliver all DMCA takedown notices to PollRepo’s designated Copyright Agent at copyright@pollrepo.com.
